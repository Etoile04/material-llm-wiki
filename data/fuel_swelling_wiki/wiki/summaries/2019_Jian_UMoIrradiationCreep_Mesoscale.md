# Summary: 2019_Jian_UMoIrradiationCreep_Mesoscale

**Source:** Jian Xiaobin, Yan Feng, Kong Xiangzhe, Ding Shurong, Nuclear Materials and Energy 21 (2019) 100706

## English

This study investigates the effects of U-Mo irradiation creep coefficient on mesoscale mechanical behavior in U-Mo/Al monolithic fuel plates using 3D finite element simulations. A mechanistic fission gas swelling model dependent on external hydrostatic pressure is adopted, replacing simpler empirical correlations. The mesoscale normal stress model accounts for bubble volume fraction, bubble pressure, and surface tension contributions to the effective stress in the porous fuel skeleton. Five irradiation creep coefficients (10–2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa) are systematically evaluated against RERTR-9A mini-plate experimental data. The optimal creep coefficient for U-10Mo is identified as 2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa, consistent with previous 3D studies using mechanistic swelling models. Key findings include: (1) increasing creep coefficient from 10 to 1000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa reduces maximum mesoscale normal stress by ~52% but only increases creep strain by ~5%; (2) further increase to 2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa raises mesoscale stress by ~9% while creep strain surges by ~219%; (3) surface tension contribution to mesoscale stress is negligible (<5%); (4) dangerous regions are located near fuel foil corners where maximum mesoscale tensile stresses occur. The study concludes that irradiation creep performance must be optimized — extremely large creep coefficients are undesirable due to creep-induced damage degrading the fuel skeleton strength.

## 中文

本研究采用三维有限元模拟，系统研究了U-Mo辐照蠕变系数对U-Mo/Al单层燃料板中介观力学行为的影响。采用考虑外部静水压力依赖性的力学裂变气体肿胀模型，替代了简化的经验公式。介观正应力模型综合考虑了气泡体积分数、气泡压力和表面张力对多孔燃料骨架有效应力的贡献。针对RERTR-9A迷你板的实验数据，评估了五个辐照蠕变系数（10–2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa）。U-10Mo的最优蠕变系数确定为2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa。主要发现包括：（1）蠕变系数从10增至1000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa时，最大介观正应力降低约52%，而蠕变应变仅增加约5%；（2）进一步增至2000 × 10⁻³¹ m³/MPa时，介观应力反而增加约9%，蠕变应变急剧增加约219%；（3）表面张力对介观应力的贡献可忽略（<5%）；（4）危险区域位于燃料箔边缘转角处。研究表明，辐照蠕变性能需要优化，过大的蠕变系数会因蠕变损伤削弱燃料骨架强度。

**Keywords:** U-Mo, irradiation creep, mesoscale normal stress, fission gas swelling, bubble volume fraction, monolithic fuel plate, fuel fracture
