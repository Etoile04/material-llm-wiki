# Summary: 2016_Carmack_FCCI_MFF3_Metallography

**Source:** Carmack, W.J. et al., "Metallography and fuel cladding chemical interaction in fast flux test facility irradiated metallic U-10Zr MFF-3 and MFF-5 fuel pins," *J. Nucl. Mater.* 473 (2016) 167–177.

## English Summary

This paper presents post-irradiation examination (PIE) results from the MFF-3 and MFF-5 assemblies irradiated in the Fast Flux Test Facility (FFTF), which contained full-length (91.4 cm) U-10Zr metallic fuel pins clad in HT9 steel. These experiments are critical for comparing metallic fuel performance in a large-scale fast reactor (FFTF) versus the smaller EBR-II test reactor. Both assemblies operated to high burnup (>10 at%) with no cladding breaches. Key findings include: (1) axial fuel column growth was remarkably low (~1.6% for MFF-3, ~3.6% for MFF-5), significantly less than the ~7% observed in prior FFTF IFR-1 tests, potentially due to higher operating temperatures; (2) cladding profilometry revealed peak diametral strains of 0.5% (MFF-5) and 1.3% (MFF-3) with a distinctive double-peak pattern in the higher-burnup pin; (3) fission gas release was 61% for MFF-3 and 79% for MFF-5; and (4) fuel-cladding chemical interaction (FCCI) was notably less severe than in EBR-II X447 pins at comparable burnup and cladding temperatures, because FFTF's chopped cosine flux profile places peak burnup at lower fuel column positions where temperatures are reduced, thereby limiting lanthanide diffusion to the cladding interface. Maximum FCCI depth was ~150 μm in MFF-3 at X/L=0.98. These results demonstrate that reactor flux profile geometry significantly affects FCCI formation rates.

## 中文摘要

本文报告了在快中子通量试验装置（FFTF）中辐照的MFF-3和MFF-5组件的辐照后检验结果，这些组件包含全长度（91.4 cm）U-10Zr金属燃料棒，包壳材料为HT9钢。这些实验对于比较大规模快堆（FFTF）与较小的EBR-II试验堆中金属燃料的性能至关重要。两个组件均在高燃耗（>10 at%）下运行且未发生包壳破裂。主要发现包括：（1）轴向燃料柱增长非常低（MFF-3约1.6%，MFF-5约3.6%），远低于先前FFTF IFR-1试验中观察到的约7%，可能归因于较高的运行温度；（2）包壳形貌测量显示峰值直径应变为0.5%（MFF-5）和1.3%（MFF-3），高燃耗棒呈现独特的双峰特征；（3）裂变气体释放率MFF-3为61%，MFF-5为79%；（4）在可比燃耗和包壳温度下，燃料-包壳化学相互作用（FCCI）明显轻于EBR-II X447燃料棒，因为FFTF的截断余弦通量分布将峰值燃耗置于燃料柱较低位置，温度较低，从而限制了镧系元素向包壳界面的扩散。MFF-3在X/L=0.98处最大FCCI深度约150 μm。

## Key Data
- **MFF-3:** 13.8 at% peak burnup, 643°C peak cladding temp (BOL), 726.2 EFPD
- **MFF-5:** 10.1 at% peak burnup, 651°C peak cladding temp (BOL), 503 EFPD
- **FCCI:** Max ~150 μm (MFF-3) and ~50 μm (MFF-5), significantly less than X447 at similar conditions
- **Fuel growth:** 1.6% (MFF-3), 3.6% (MFF-5)
