# Fuel-Cladding Chemical Interaction in U-Pu-Zr Metallic Fuels: A Critical Review

**Authors:** Christopher Matthews, Cetin Unal, Jack Galloway, Dennis D. Keiser Jr., Steven L. Hayes
**Journal:** Nuclear Technology, 198:3, 231-259 (2017)
**DOI:** 10.1080/00295450.2017.1323535

## English Summary

This critical review by Matthews et al. (2017) systematically decomposes fuel-cladding chemical interaction (FCCI) in U-Pu-Zr metallic fuels into three principal phenomena: (1) lanthanide production and transport, (2) fuel cracking and rind integrity, and (3) fuel/cladding inter-diffusion. The review synthesizes decades of EBR-II and FFTF irradiation data along with extensive out-of-pile diffusion couple experiments.

Lanthanide fission products, particularly neodymium and cerium (fission yields of ~7% and 8-11%, respectively), migrate down the temperature gradient via liquid-like diffusion through fission-gas-filled pores and accumulate at the fuel periphery. The zirconium-rich rind formed during fabrication (likely due to nitrogen impurities) acts as a diffusion barrier, but fuel cracking—driven by anisotropic swelling in the α-phase and thermal stresses—destroys this barrier, enabling FCCI. Nickel in cladding suppresses rind formation, while lanthanides accelerate inter-diffusion through low-temperature eutectics (Ce/Ni at 750 K, Nd/Fe). The review identifies parabolic growth kinetics (Δx = √(2tk)) with Arrhenius temperature dependence for the inter-diffusion zone, reports eutectic melting temperatures (U/Fe: 992 K, Pu/Fe: 685 K), and notes that plutonium content significantly lowers the fuel melting threshold. The paper recommends mechanistic modeling approaches including phase-field methods for inter-diffusion and XFEM/peridynamics for crack prediction, and proposes targeted experiments to validate these models.

## 中文摘要

Matthews等人（2017）的这篇综述性论文系统地将U-Pu-Zr金属燃料中的燃料-包壳化学相互作用（FCCI）分解为三个主要现象：（1）镧系元素的产生与迁移，（2）燃料开裂与锆富集层完整性，（3）燃料/包壳界面互扩散。文章综合了数十年的EBR-II和FFTF辐照数据以及大量堆外扩散偶实验结果。

镧系裂变产物（主要为钕和铈，裂变产额分别约为7%和8-11%）通过裂变气孔中的类液体扩散沿温度梯度向燃料外围迁移并积累。制造过程中形成的锆富集层（可能由氮杂质引起）起到扩散屏障作用，但燃料开裂——由α相各向异性肿胀和热应力驱动——破坏了这一屏障，使FCCI得以发生。包壳中的镍会抑制锆富集层的形成，而镧系元素通过低温共晶反应（Ce/Ni: 750 K, Nd/Fe）加速互扩散。文章指出互扩散区遵循抛物线生长动力学（Δx = √(2tk)）和阿伦尼乌斯温度依赖关系，报告了共晶熔化温度（U/Fe: 992 K, Pu/Fe: 685 K），并指出钚含量显著降低燃料熔化阈值。论文推荐采用相场方法模拟互扩散、XFEM/近场动力学预测裂纹的力学建模方法，并提出了验证这些模型的定向实验建议。

## Key Topics
- FCCI mechanism decomposition
- Lanthanide transport (liquid-phase diffusion via pores, Soret diffusion)
- Zirconium rind formation and failure
- Fuel cracking mechanics
- Diffusion couple experiments (arc-cast vs injection-cast)
- Eutectic melting temperatures
- Modeling approaches (phase-field, XFEM, peridynamics)
- Mitigation strategies (Pd doping, ODS steels, diffusion barriers)
